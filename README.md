# 🏥 PMTCT Community-Level Analysis

> Analysing disparities in Prevention of Mother-to-Child Transmission 
> (PMTCT) services across communities using PEPFAR MER data.

---

## 🧭 About This Project

This project was conducted as part of a fellowship with the Women 
Ascension Research & Policy Development (WARPD) initiative. It explores 
community-level disparities in PMTCT service delivery, focusing on HIV 
testing coverage, ART uptake, and cascade performance across communities.

The goal is to identify high-burden communities and service gaps that 
can inform targeted public health interventions.

LGA-level data was used as a proxy for community-level service access 
and health outcomes.

---

## 📌 Key Questions Investigated

- How does HIV testing coverage vary across LGAs?
- Where are HIV positive cases most concentrated?
- What patterns of disparity exist in ART coverage and PMTCT cascade?
- Which LGAs account for the highest burden of PMTCT cases?

## 💡 Key Findings

- HIV testing coverage varies significantly across LGAs, with a small 
number of LGAs accounting for the majority of tested individuals
- Approximately 80% of PMTCT burden is concentrated in 18 high-impact LGAs
- ART coverage remains consistently high across most LGAs
- Significant disparities exist in case detection and service access
